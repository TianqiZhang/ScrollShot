Complete Flow

Step-by-Step Lifecycle
1
Activate
Hotkey or tray click → screen dims, crosshair cursor appears
2
Select Area
Click and drag to draw a rectangle. Selected area is highlighted, rest stays dimmed.
3
Branch Point — Instant or Scroll
Enter → instant capture, jump to step 6
Scroll (mouse wheel / trackpad) → enters scroll-capture mode (step 4)
Esc → cancel
4
Scroll Capture Mode
Overlay dims except the selected area. User scrolls naturally.
A live preview strip appears on the side, growing as content is captured.
Direction indicator shows ↓ or → based on scroll direction.
5
Finish Scrolling
Press Enter or click Done ✓ button → finalize stitch
Press Esc → discard and cancel
6
Preview & Edit
Lightweight editor opens with the result.
Timeline-style strip: trim head/tail, cut middle sections.
Crop tool for final framing.
7
Save / Copy / Discard
Save → PNG to configured folder
Copy → to clipboard
Discard → throw away
