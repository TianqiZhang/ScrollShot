How It Works
On the timeline strip: Click-drag vertically (or horizontally) to select a range. The selected range highlights in red. Press Delete or click ✂️ to cut.
On the main viewport: In trim mode, click-drag directly on the image to mark a region for removal. The timeline strip mirrors the selection.
Trim handles at the edges still work for quick head/tail trimming.
Multiple cuts supported — each shown as a red band on the timeline. All reversible with Ctrl+Z.

Interaction Summary

All Editor Actions
Trim Head/Tail Drag orange handles at the edges of the timeline strip. Live viewport update.
Free-Cut Click-drag on timeline or image to select a range. Delete key or ✂️ button to cut. Multiple cuts allowed. Red band on timeline shows each cut zone.
Chrome Toggle Include/exclude fixed top/bottom/left/right regions from the final output.
Crop Rectangular crop overlay on the main viewport. Drag corners/edges to adjust.
Navigate Scroll to zoom, drag to pan, Fit/1:1 buttons. Click timeline position to jump viewport.
Undo/Redo Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y. All operations reversible until Save.
